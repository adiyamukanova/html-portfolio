The best movies I have ever seen

Top 3

"Avatar" (2009)

Set in the lush, alien world of Pandora, the film follows paraplegic ex-Marine Jake Sully, who is chosen to participate in the Avatar Program, which allows him to control an avatar body. As he becomes closer to the indigenous Na'vi people, Jake is torn between helping them and his mission to extract resources from Pandora.

"The Dark Knight" (2008)

Batman faces a new threat in Gotham City as the Joker, a sadistic criminal mastermind, emerges with a plan to create chaos and take down Batman. The film delves into the morality of crime-fighting and the psychological battle between Batman and the Joker.

"Avengers: Endgame" (2019)

The Avengers assemble to undo the devastation caused by Thanos, the powerful villain who wiped out half of all life in the universe using the Infinity Stones. The team devises a plan to travel through time, collect the stones, and restore balance.
